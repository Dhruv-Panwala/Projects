Lining shipping is the solution of delivering products by means of high-capacity, ocean-going ships that transportation normal routes on fixed schedules. There are approximately 400 liner services, the majority of sailing weekly, in procedure today. Lining vessels, mainly in the kind of containerships as well as roll-on/roll-off ships, carry about 60 percent of the items by value moved internationally by sea every year.
